# Video Game Market Analysis Presentation

## Slide 1: Executive Summary
- Analyzed global video game sales data from 1980-2020
- Key findings on regional preferences, platform trends, and publisher performance
- Strategic recommendations for new game development

## Slide 2: Dataset Overview
- Dataset contains 16,598 games across multiple platforms and genres
- Time period: 1980 to 2020 (with most data from 2000s-2010s)
- Key metrics: NA, EU, JP, and Other regional sales

## Slide 3: Regional Genre Preferences - North America
- Top genres:
  1. Action (861.77M sales)
  2. Sports (670.09M sales)
  3. Shooter (575.16M sales)
- Action and Sports dominate the NA market

## Slide 4: Regional Genre Preferences - Japan
- Top genres:
  1. Role-Playing (350.29M sales)
  2. Action (158.65M sales)
  3. Sports (134.76M sales)
- **Key insight: RPGs significantly more popular in Japan**

## Slide 5: Platform Lifecycle Analysis
- PS3 peak around 2011-2012, followed by decline
- PS4 showed strong growth starting in 2013
- Newer platforms typically follow adoption curve: slow start, peak, decline

## Slide 6: Publisher Hit Rates
- Top performers by average sales per game:
  1. Nintendo (2.56M avg per game)
  2. Sony Computer Entertainment Europe (1.59M)
  3. Microsoft Game Studios (1.30M)
- Nintendo shows highest efficiency in game releases

## Slide 7: Top Genre-Platform Combinations
- Best performing combinations:
  1. Shooter-NES (5.09M avg)
  2. Role-Playing-GB (4.20M avg)
  3. Adventure-GB (3.43M avg)
- Retro platforms showing high averages due to classics

## Slide 8: Strategic Recommendations
1. For Japanese market: Focus on RPG genre
2. For North American market: Focus on Action/Sports/Shooter genres
3. Consider partnerships with Nintendo-style publishers for quality consistency
4. Target newer platforms during their growth phase

## Slide 9: Risk Assessment
- Market trends change rapidly
- Platform lifecycles vary
- Regional preferences may evolve
- Need to balance innovation with proven formulas

## Slide 10: Final Recommendation
- **Recommended Strategy**: Develop an Action/RPG hybrid for PS4/Xbox One era platforms
- Target both NA and JP markets with culturally adapted elements
- Focus on high-quality production similar to Nintendo's approach
- Launch timing during platform growth phase for maximum reach
